Welcome to our page commemorating the 25th Kargil Vijay Diwas, celebrated on July 26, 2024, at New Horizon College of Engineering, Bengaluru. Organized by the NCC cadets and led by Mr. Jayasheel Kumar, this event honors the valor and sacrifices of the soldiers who fought in the Kargil War.

AO Maj. Keerthi Raghuvaran G
Lt. Col. Durga Prasad Nanda

The ceremony began with the NCC cadets formally welcoming the Chief Guests, including CO Lt. Col. Durga Prasad Nanda and AO Maj. Keerthi Raghuvaran G. A cadet delivered an insightful speech on the historical significance of Kargil Vijay Diwas. A candle-lighting ceremony followed, honoring the martyrs with a somber and reflective atmosphere.

Distinguished guests, including Principal Dr. Manjunatha and Group Captain Tarun Batra, delivered speeches emphasizing the bravery of the soldiers. A stirring patriotic song was performed, and a cadet spoke about the importance of remembering the sacrifices made during the Kargil conflict. A compelling video presentation provided a detailed overview of the war, showcasing the challenges and triumphs of the soldiers.

The event concluded with a Vote of Thanks, expressing gratitude to all participants and attendees. The singing of the National Anthem united everyone in a moment of national pride. The 25th Kargil Vijay Diwas celebration at NHCE was a profound tribute to our soldiers' bravery, filling everyone with pride, gratitude, and a deep sense of patriotism.

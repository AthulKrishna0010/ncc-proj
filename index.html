About NCC

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is the youth wing of the Indian Armed Forces with its headquarters in New Delhi, India. It is open to school and college students on voluntary basis as a Tri-Services Organization, comprising the Army, the Navy and the Air Force. Cadets are given basic military training in small arms and drill. Officers and cadets have no liability for active military service once they complete their course.

NCC at NHCE

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) at New Horizon College of Engineering plays a vital role in shaping young students into disciplined and responsible citizens. The NCC unit offers a blend of military training, leadership development, and community service. Our aim is to foster the qualities of patriotism, unity, and self-discipline among our cadets.

Founded in [2021], the NCC 7 KAR Battalion unit at New Horizon College of Engineering has a proud history of participation in various national and state-level events. Our cadets are actively involved in social service, disaster relief, and public awareness campaigns, contributing to the welfare of the society
